About Me

Hi, my name is Destiney! I am a premedical students at Arizona State University, studying Biomedical Sciences and Statistics. I spend most of my time studying, reading, doing yoga, hiking, traveling the world, and of course taking pictures.

I first became interested photography during high school when I bought my first digital camera. As I practiced with various lighting and poses, I fell more in love with it. After a year on the digital camera, I finally invested in my first "big girl camera" and took the world by storm. Now I live to catch the smallest details of the big moments in your life.
